Features

Everything You Need

A complete platform built for modern online assessments

Live Countdown Timer

Real-time exam timer with color-coded warnings. Auto-submits when time expires — no manual intervention needed.

Randomized Questions

Questions are shuffled on every attempt, preventing answer sharing and ensuring exam integrity.

Instant Auto-Evaluation

Answers are evaluated server-side the moment the exam is submitted. Results appear instantly.

Deep Analytics

Admin dashboard shows pass rates, average scores, highest/lowest performers per exam.

Anti-Cheat Protection

Tab-switch detection, page-refresh warnings, and server-side answer validation keep exams fair.

Fully Responsive

Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Students can take exams from any device.
